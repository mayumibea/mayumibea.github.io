Hello!

I'm Beatriz Mayumi, a character designer and illustrator living in São Paulo, Brazil. Through experience with publishing and editorial work in addition to concept art for animation and advertisement, I have worked with Albert Whitman & Co, BUCK Design, Lobo.cx along many others amazing people, and collaborated in projects for clients such as Facebook, Apple TV and Disney Parks. I'm always interested in telling stories that don't relies on stereotypes, shows diversity in people and embraces all kinds of cultures and backgrounds.

If you want to reach out regarding upcoming projects or would like to know more about my work send a message at mayumibea@gmail.com.
